KMCT COLLEGE

KMCT College of Engineering was established in the year 2001 by the KMCT (Kunhitharuvai Memorial Charitable Trust), based in Kozhikode, Kerala. It is affiliated to AICTE and University of Calicut. It is currently affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, from 2015 onwards.

KMCT College of Engineering
